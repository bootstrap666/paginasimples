About

Received the B.E.E. degree from the Federal University of Paraná (UFPR), Curitiba, Brazil, the M.Sc. degree in electrical engineering from CPGEI/UTFPR, and the Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from UFSC, Brazil, in 2002, 2005, and 2014, respectively. He joined the Department of Electronics and Computing, Federal University of Santa Maria (UFSM), Santa Maria, Brazil, in 2016. He is currently a Professor of Computing Engineering at UFSM. His recent research interests are in statistical signal processing, including linear and nonlinear adaptive filtering, image processing, and machine learning.
